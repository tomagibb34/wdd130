History

We have an extensive history of safe white water rafting excursions. We have been in business for 35 years and have many testimonials regarding our pursuit of successful, and complete one through five day trips on a variety of class one through class five white water floating environments within our jurisdiction. We have had many overnight trips that have been very well documented and provide an extremely high observable discovery of the environment and how we can better enhance and fulfill all of your white water rafting dreams and goals.
